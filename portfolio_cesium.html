When working in ecommerce I realized how valuable 3D models are for showing products to customers. This led me to create Folio3D.

Folio3D allows creatives to showcase their work in an online 3D environment. Imagine a WIX or Squarespace, but instead of building 2D portfolio websites, you're constructing immersive worlds.

Current workflow to get 3D media hosted online WITHOUT Folio3D

Folio3D takes this mess and turns it into three simple steps:

  1. Choose a starting template
  2. Upload or scan 3D models
  3. Place the models within the template

Users can utilize templates and artificial intelligence to craft a personalized space for displaying their products, architecture, paintings, sculptures, music, UX design, and more. Folio3D allows anyone to build a captivating virtual portfolio without writing a single line of code.
